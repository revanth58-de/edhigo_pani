## üöÄ Getting Started

### 1. Installation
*   **Android/iOS:** Download "FarmConnect" from the App Store or Play Store.
*   **Web:** Visit `app.farmconnect.com`.

### 2. Launch & Language
*   Open the app.
*   **Screen 01:** View the Splash Screen.
*   **Screen 02:** Select your preferred language (English, Telugu, Hindi). *Voice guidance is available in all supported languages.*

### 3. Login & Authentication
*   **Screen 03:** Enter your Mobile Number.
*   **Screen 04:** Enter the 4-digit OTP sent to your phone.
*   **Screen 05:** Select your Role:
    *   **Farmer:** To hire workers.
    *   **Worker:** To find work individually.
    *   **Group Leader:** To manage and deploy a team of workers.

## üë®‚Äçüåæ Manufacturer / Farmer Flow
*For users looking to hire labor.*

### Step 1: Post a Job
1.  **Home (Screen 06):** Tap "HIRE WORKERS".
2.  **Profile (Screen 07):** Verify your details (optional).
3.  **Select Workers (Screen 08):** Choose the number of workers needed, work type (e.g., Harvest), and duration.
4.  **Request Sent (Screen 09):** The system searches for nearby available workers.
5.  **Accepted (Screen 10):** View profiles of workers who accepted.

### Step 2: Manage Attendance
1.  **Arrival (Screen 11):** Receive alerts when workers are nearby (Geofence triggers).
2.  **Check-In (Screen 12):** Show your **QR Code** to workers. They scan it to start the "Shift Clock".
3.  **Monitor (Screen 13):** Dashboard shows active workers, time elapsed, and current cost.

### Step 3: Complete & Pay
1.  **Check-Out (Screen 14):** Show **QR Out** code to stop the clock.
2.  **Payment (Screen 15):** Review summary (Hours √ó Rate). Pay via UPI/Cash.
3.  **Rate (Screen 16):** Rate the worker's performance (Happy/Neutral/Sad).

## üë∑ Individual Worker Flow
*For individuals seeking daily wage work.*

### Step 1: Find Work
1.  **Home (Screen 17):** Set status to "ONLINE".
2.  **Job Offer (Screen 18):** Receive a popup with Job Type, Distance, and Pay.
3.  **Accept:** Tap "ACCEPT JOB".
4.  **Navigation (Screen 19):** Follow the map to the farm location.

### Step 2: Work Session
1.  **Check-In (Screen 20):** Open scanner and scan the **Farmer's QR Code**.
2.  **Confirmation (Screen 21):** "Attendance Marked" success screen.
3.  **Status (Screen 22):** View "Work In Progress" timer and earnings.

### Step 3: Finish
1.  **Check-Out:** Scan the Farmer's **QR Out** code.
2.  **Rate Farmer (Screen 23):** Rate your experience with the employer.
3.  **Profile (Screen 24):** Check total earnings and reputation.

## üë• Group Leader Flow
*For leaders managing a team (e.g., 10-50 workers).*

### Step 1: Form a Group
1.  **Home (Screen 25):** Tap "START GROUP".
2.  **Setup (Screen 26):** Enter Member Count (e.g., 15) and Payment Mode (Per Head / Per Acre).
3.  **Go Live:** Your group is now visible to Farmers on the Live Map.

### Step 2: Bulk Management
1.  **Job Offer (Screen 27):** Accept large contracts suitable for your group size.
2.  **Group Check-In (Screen 28):** Display **YOUR Leader QR Code**. The *Farmer* scans this single code to check in all 15 workers at once.
3.  **Confirmation (Screen 29):** System logs start time for all members.

### Step 3: Settlement
1.  **Finish:** Farmer scans Leader QR to end session.
2.  **Rate (Screen 30):** Rate the Farmer.
3.  **Payout:** Lump sum or individual distribution based on settings.

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: What if I lose internet/GPS?**
A: The app stores the last known state. Attendance requires an active connection at the moment of scanning, but tracking works offline and syncs later.

**Q: Can I change my role?**
A: Yes, logout and re-verify your phone number to select a different role for the new session.

**Q: How are payments handled?**
A: The app calculates the amount. Actual money transfer happens via UPI apps (PhonePe/GPay) or Cash, recorded manually in the app for receipt generation.
